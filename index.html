Hydroponic farming is growing vegetables without soil, also called soilless farming. In hydroponic farming, the roots of the plant grow in liquid nutrient solution. Hydroponically grown vegetables are better in quality & taste as the farming is done under controlled environment without usage of chemicals or pesticides. As hydroponic farming can be done anywhere, there is no limitation of space & the growth of vegetables is also very fast. As the environment is controlled for this farming, the fresh hydroponic vegetables can be grown anytime of the year. There is minimal risk of infection of vegetables due to pests or insects. Hydroponic farming is environmentally friendly & thus helps in lowering the waste.
